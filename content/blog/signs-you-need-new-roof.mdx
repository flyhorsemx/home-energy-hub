Your roof is your home's first line of defense. Here are seven signs it may be time to replace it. For a detailed look at how long different materials last, see our guide to [roof lifespan by material](/blog/how-long-does-roof-last).

## 1. Your Roof Is Over 20 Years Old

Most asphalt shingle roofs last **20–25 years**. If yours is older, it's living on borrowed time — even if it looks fine from the street.

## 2. Shingles Are Curling or Buckling

Curled or buckled shingles are a sign of moisture damage or improper installation. This allows water infiltration that can rot your decking.

## 3. You're Finding Granules in the Gutters

Asphalt shingles shed granules as they age. If your gutters are full of dark, sand-like debris, your shingles are losing their UV protection.

## 4. Daylight Through the Roof Boards

If you can see daylight through your attic, so can water and cold air. This is an urgent repair situation.

## 5. Sagging Roof Deck

A sagging roof deck indicates trapped moisture weakening the supporting structure. This can become a collapse risk.

## 6. Rising Energy Bills Without Explanation

A failing roof with compromised insulation causes your [HVAC system](/hvac) to work harder. If your bills spiked for no obvious reason, your roof may be to blame.

## 7. Storm Damage

After any major storm, have your roof inspected. Insurance companies typically cover storm damage — but you must file within the claim window.

<ProWarningAlert category="roofing">
  Roof inspections and repairs involve working at height with physical risk. Even experienced DIYers can misjudge structural integrity. Get a professional assessment before climbing up.
</ProWarningAlert>

## Repair vs. Replace: How to Decide

For more guidance, see our full [roof repair vs replacement decision framework](/blog/roof-repair-vs-replacement) and [roof replacement cost guide](/blog/roof-replacement-cost) for current pricing data.

| Situation | Recommendation |
|-----------|----------------|
| Damage on &lt;30% of roof | Spot repair |
| Roof 15+ years old + multiple issues | Full replacement |
| Active leak with interior damage | Emergency repair + inspect for replacement |
| Planning to sell home | Full replacement (higher ROI) |
