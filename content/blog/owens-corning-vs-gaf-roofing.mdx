GAF and Owens Corning together account for roughly **80% of all asphalt shingles sold in the United States**. Both make quality products. The real question is which offers better protection for your specific situation — and that comes down to warranty, contractor programs, and regional availability.

## The Flagship Products Head-to-Head

### GAF Timberline HDZ vs. Owens Corning Duration

These are the best-selling shingles in America — most residential reroof jobs use one or the other.

| Spec | GAF Timberline HDZ | OC Duration |
|---|---|---|
| Wind rating | 130 mph (with 6 nails) | 130 mph |
| Impact rating | Class 4 (HDZ RS) | Class 4 (available) |
| Algae resistance | StainGuard Plus 25 yr | TruDefinition 10 yr (standard) |
| Weight | ~235 lbs/square | ~240 lbs/square |
| Granule bond | LayerLock | SureNail strip |
| Warranty | Lifetime (ShingleMate required) | Lifetime (OC starter required) |

Both are excellent shingles. The main practical difference: **GAF's LayerLock** technology provides enhanced wind resistance without requiring 6-nail installation — useful in high-wind regions. **Owens Corning's SureNail** technology centers nails in the fastening zone for consistent installation quality.

### Premium Line: GAF Camelot vs. OC TruDefinition Duration Designer

For high-end projects wanting slate or shake aesthetics:
- **GAF Camelot II**: Up to 110 mph, Lifetime warranty, $6–$10/sq ft
- **OC Berkshire**: Up to 130 mph, Lifetime warranty, $7–$11/sq ft
- **GAF Grand Sequoia**: Thick-cut architectural, $7–$12/sq ft

Premium lines are aesthetically excellent from both brands. Price and appearance preference matter more than performance differences.

## The Warranty Deep Dive

Shingle warranties have more caveats than most homeowners realize. Here's what actually matters.

### GAF Warranty Tiers

| Program | Who Offers It | Covers | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| Standard Limited | Any contractor | Shingles (manufacturing defects) | Lifetime (prorated after 10 yr) |
| Silver Pledge | GAF Certified contractor | Shingles + workmanship | 25 years |
| Golden Pledge | Master Elite contractor only | Shingles + full workmanship | 50 years |

**Master Elite** contractors represent the top 3% of GAF-certified roofers. They're vetted, insured, and must pass ongoing requirements. A Golden Pledge warranty from a Master Elite contractor is one of the strongest roofing warranties available — and it transfers to future buyers.

### Owens Corning Warranty Tiers

| Program | Who Offers It | Covers | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic Limited | Any contractor | Shingles only | Lifetime (prorated) |
| Preferred Protection | Preferred Contractor | Shingles + workmanship | 25 years |
| Platinum Protection | Preferred Contractor | Full system + workmanship | 50 years |

OC's Preferred Contractor program is less selective than GAF's Master Elite. The warranty coverage is comparable, but GAF's contractor vetting is more rigorous.

## Color and Aesthetic Options

### GAF
Timberline HDZ offers 35+ colors including popular charcoal, weathered wood, and barkwood tones. The Camelot and Grand Canyon lines provide premium architectural profiles. GAF's color simulation tools at gaf.com are well-regarded for visualizing results.

### Owens Corning
Duration and TruDefinition Duration shingles come in 50+ colors, with distinctive "Oakridge" tones that tend toward richer, more saturated hues. The Berkshire and Woodcrest collections offer high-end profiles. OC's website visualization tool is also good.

**Bottom line on color:** Both brands have excellent palettes. Pick colors based on your home's exterior, not brand loyalty.

## Algae Resistance

Algae streaking (those black stains on roofs) is a major concern in humid climates. Both brands offer treated shingles, but the warranty lengths differ:

- **GAF StainGuard Plus:** 25-year algae warranty on qualifying shingles
- **OC Duration:** 10-year standard algae warranty (TruDefinition Duration — 25 years)

If algae is a concern in your climate (Gulf Coast, Southeast, Pacific Northwest), look for the 25-year or lifetime algae resistance options in either brand.

## Regional Availability and Installer Expertise

**GAF** has slightly broader distribution and more Master Elite contractors in most markets. Finding a GAF-certified installer is easier in rural areas.

**Owens Corning** has strong distribution in the Midwest and Northeast, where many established local roofers have worked with OC products for decades.

**The practical advice:** Ask your top roofer candidates which brand they prefer and why. An experienced contractor who has installed 500 Timberline roofs will do a better job with Timberline than they will with Duration, even if Duration is slightly superior on paper.

## The Honest Answer

For most homeowners, the **installer choice matters more than the brand**. A Master Elite GAF contractor installing Timberline HDZ with a Golden Pledge warranty is a better outcome than a mediocre contractor installing the "better" Owens Corning shingles.

Get 3+ quotes. Ask about their certification level with their preferred brand. Check their Google and BBB reviews. The contractor vetting process is where the real work happens.
